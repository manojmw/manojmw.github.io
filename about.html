Manoj M Wagle

PhD Candidate

The University of Sydney

I am a PhD candidate in the Faculty of Science at The University of Sydney and the Children's Medical Research Institute. I conduct my research in the School of Mathematics and Statistics (USydney) and Manolis Kellis Lab (Massachusetts Institute of Technology).

I am fascinated by the interdisciplinary potential of Biology and Artificial Intelligence to improve our understanding of complex cellular activities and uncover underlying mechanisms of disease pathogenesis. My current research focuses on developing and applying computational approaches, particularly leveraging Deep Learning techniques to facilitate the analysis of high-throughput biological data. Specifically, I am interested in applying these approaches to answer questions in the field of Single-cell omics, Neuroscience and Precision medicine.

Outside of research, I love spending time in nature and with my dogs, hiking, practicing yoga/meditation, Shōtōkan martial arts, badminton or simply experimenting in the kitchen :)
